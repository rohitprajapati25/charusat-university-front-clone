CHARUSAT HISTORY

CHARUSAT (Charotar University of Science and Technology) is a prominent private university in Gujarat, India, established under Gujarat Private Universities Act, 2009. It is located in Changa, a village in the Anand district, and is a hub for higher education, innovation, and research.
